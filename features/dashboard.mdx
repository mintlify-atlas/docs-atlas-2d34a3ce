The Dashboard is your home screen in Recur, providing a complete overview of your recurring expenses with interactive charts, filtering options, and customizable views.

## Dashboard Layout

When you open Recur, you'll see:

1. **Personalized Greeting**: A time-of-day greeting with your name
2. **Add Subscription Button**: Orange + icon in the top-right corner
3. **View Mode Toggle**: Switch between Monthly and Yearly views
4. **Chart Visualization**: Animated chart showing your total expenses
5. **List Filter**: Filter chip to view specific lists or all subscriptions
6. **Subscription Cards**: Scrollable list of all your subscriptions

## Chart Visualization

The chart displays your total recurring costs with a beautiful animated visualization.

### What the Chart Shows

- **Total Amount**: Your combined subscription costs in the selected view mode
- **Currency Symbol**: Displayed using your default currency
- **View Mode**: Either Monthly or Yearly totals

### How Totals Are Calculated

Recur automatically converts subscription costs based on your selected view:

- **Monthly View**: Yearly subscriptions are divided by 12
- **Yearly View**: Monthly subscriptions are multiplied by 12

For example, if you have:
- Netflix: $15.99/month
- Adobe Creative Cloud: $599.88/year

**Monthly View** shows: $15.99 + ($599.88 ÷ 12) = $65.99/month

**Yearly View** shows: ($15.99 × 12) + $599.88 = $791.76/year

## Switching View Modes

Toggle between monthly and yearly expense views.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the view toggle">
    Find the **Monthly** / **Yearly** segmented control below the greeting.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your preferred view">
    Tap either **Monthly** or **Yearly** to switch the view mode.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View updated totals">
    The chart and all subscription cards update to reflect the selected frequency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your view mode preference affects only the display—it doesn't change the actual billing frequency of your subscriptions.
</Note>

## Filtering Subscriptions

Filter your Dashboard to show specific lists or unassigned subscriptions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the filter dialog">
    Tap the **List: All** chip below the chart (it shows your current filter).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a filter option">
    Choose from:
    - **All**: Show all subscriptions (default)
    - **Unassigned**: Show only subscriptions without a list
    - **Your Lists**: Any custom lists you've created
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View filtered results">
    The Dashboard updates to show only subscriptions matching your filter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filter Behavior

- **Chart Updates**: The total cost reflects only the filtered subscriptions
- **Drag-and-Drop**: Reordering is disabled when filtering by a specific list (only available in "All" view)
- **Empty State**: If no subscriptions match your filter, a helpful message appears

## Subscription Cards

Each subscription appears as a card showing:

- **Icon/Avatar**: Emoji or initials
- **Service Name**: The subscription name
- **Cost**: Amount in your currency, adjusted for the current view mode
- **Frequency**: Original billing cycle (monthly/yearly)

Tap any card to edit the subscription details.

## Personalized Greeting

The Dashboard greets you based on the time of day:

- **Morning** (before 12 PM): "Good morning"
- **Afternoon** (12 PM - 5 PM): "Good afternoon"
- **Evening** (after 5 PM): "Good evening"

Your name (set during onboarding) appears next to the greeting with a wave emoji.

## Empty State

When you first use Recur or have no subscriptions:

- A message prompts you to add your first subscription
- The total shows $0.00
- The chart displays an empty state

Simply tap the + button to get started!

## Refreshing Data

The Dashboard automatically refreshes when:

- You return to the app from Settings
- You add, edit, or delete a subscription
- You create or delete a list

No manual refresh is needed—your data is always up to date.
