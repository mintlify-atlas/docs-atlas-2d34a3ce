Recur is built with privacy as a core principle. Your subscription data is personal financial information, and it never leaves your device.

## No Accounts, No Cloud

Recur doesn't require you to create an account or sign in. There are no servers collecting your data, no cloud sync services, and no third-party integrations.

<Info>
  **Your data stays on your device.** When you add subscriptions, set preferences, or customize lists, everything is stored locally using secure on-device storage.
</Info>

## What This Means for You

### Complete Privacy
- **No tracking**: Recur doesn't include analytics, telemetry, or tracking SDKs
- **No data collection**: We don't collect, access, or transmit any of your personal information
- **No third parties**: Your subscription data is never shared with advertisers, analytics companies, or anyone else

### Instant Access
- **No sign-up required**: Start tracking subscriptions immediately after downloading
- **Offline-first**: The app works completely offline—no internet connection needed
- **No permissions**: Recur doesn't request camera, contacts, location, or any other device permissions

### Data Ownership
- **You're in control**: Your data lives only on your device
- **No vendor lock-in**: Since there's no cloud account, you're never locked into a service
- **True privacy**: No one—including the app developers—can access your subscription data

<Note>
  Recur is open-source software licensed under GNU GPLv3. You can review the entire codebase to verify the privacy guarantees.
</Note>

## Trade-offs

While the privacy-first approach has significant benefits, there are some trade-offs to be aware of:

### No Cross-Device Sync
Because your data is stored only on your device, it won't automatically sync to other phones or tablets. If you use multiple devices, you'll need to manually track subscriptions on each one.

### No Cloud Backups
Recur doesn't back up your data to the cloud. If you delete the app or lose your device, your subscription data will be lost. See [Data Storage](/features/data-storage) for more details.

### Currency is Set Once
During onboarding, you choose a default currency. This setting cannot be changed later to maintain data consistency, since all subscriptions use the same currency throughout the app.

## Privacy Policy

Recur includes a privacy policy that reinforces these commitments:

- **No data collection**: The app does not collect, sell, or share personal data
- **Local storage only**: All information is stored on your device
- **No third-party services**: No analytics, advertising, or tracking integrations
- **No special permissions**: The app doesn't request device permissions

You can review the full privacy policy within the app under **Settings → Privacy Policy**.

## Open Source Transparency

Recur is developed as an open-source project by the YeetFactory GitHub organization. The complete source code is publicly available, allowing anyone to:

- Verify privacy claims by reviewing the codebase
- Audit data handling and storage mechanisms
- Contribute improvements or security fixes
- Build and distribute their own versions

This transparency ensures that privacy commitments aren't just promises—they're verifiable facts.
