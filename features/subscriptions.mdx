---
title: Managing Subscriptions
description: Add, edit, delete, and reorder your recurring subscriptions
---

Recur makes it easy to track all your recurring expenses in one place. You can add subscriptions with custom details, edit them as needed, and organize them with drag-and-drop reordering.

## Adding a Subscription

Create a new subscription to start tracking a recurring expense.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Subscription dialog">
    Tap the orange **+** button in the top-right corner of the Dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter subscription details">
    Fill in the following information:
    - **Service Name**: Enter the name of the service (e.g., Netflix, Spotify)
    - **Icon**: Tap the avatar to select an emoji icon, or leave it to use initials
    - **List**: (Optional) Assign the subscription to a list for organization
    - **Frequency**: Select either Monthly or Yearly billing cycle
    - **Amount**: Enter the cost in your default currency
    - **Start Date**: Choose when the subscription begins
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the subscription">
    Tap **Save Subscription** to add it to your Dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you haven't created any lists yet, the List selection won't appear. You can create lists from the [Manage Lists](/features/lists) screen in Settings.
</Note>

## Editing a Subscription

Update subscription details at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a subscription card">
    Tap on any subscription card in your Dashboard to open the edit dialog.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Modify details">
    Update any field including the name, icon, list assignment, frequency, amount, or start date.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Tap **Update** to apply your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a Subscription

Remove subscriptions you no longer need to track.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the subscription">
    Tap the subscription card to open the edit dialog.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete the subscription">
    Tap the delete button (trash icon) in the dialog.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Confirm that you want to remove the subscription. This action cannot be undone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a subscription is permanent. All data for that subscription will be removed from your device.
</Warning>

## Reordering Subscriptions

Customize the order of your subscriptions with drag-and-drop functionality.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View all subscriptions">
    Make sure you're viewing **All** subscriptions (not filtered by a specific list). Reordering is only available in the All view.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Drag to reorder">
    Press and hold any subscription card, then drag it to your desired position in the list.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Release to save">
    Release the card to drop it in the new position. Your custom order is saved automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Drag-and-drop reordering is disabled when filtering by a specific list. Switch to the "All" filter to reorder your subscriptions.
</Note>

## Subscription Details

Each subscription includes:

- **Name and Icon**: Custom emoji or initials-based avatar
- **Amount**: Cost in your default currency
- **Frequency**: Monthly or yearly billing cycle
- **List Assignment**: Optional categorization (Work, Personal, etc.)
- **Start Date**: When the subscription began

The Dashboard automatically calculates your total monthly or yearly costs based on the view mode you select.
