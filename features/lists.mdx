---
title: Lists and Organization
description: Organize subscriptions into custom lists and manage unassigned items
---

Lists help you organize subscriptions into categories like "Work", "Entertainment", or "Personal". Lists are completely optional—subscriptions work perfectly fine without them.

## Creating a List

Organize your subscriptions by creating custom lists.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Manage Lists">
    Open **Settings** from the bottom tab bar, then tap **Manage Lists**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add a new list">
    Tap the **Add New List** button with the dashed border.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter list details">
    Type a name for your list (e.g., "Entertainment", "Work Tools", "Fitness").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the list">
    Tap **Add List** to create it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a List

Rename an existing list.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Manage Lists">
    Navigate to **Settings** > **Manage Lists**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select edit">
    Tap the pencil icon next to the list you want to rename.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update the name">
    Enter the new name and tap **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a List

Remove lists you no longer need.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Manage Lists">
    Navigate to **Settings** > **Manage Lists**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delete the list">
    Tap the trash icon next to the list you want to delete.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Confirm that you want to delete the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  When you delete a list, all subscriptions assigned to that list become **unassigned**. The subscriptions themselves are not deleted—they simply have no list assignment.
</Note>

## Assigning Subscriptions to Lists

You can assign subscriptions to lists when adding or editing them.

### During Creation

When adding a new subscription, select a list from the **List** dropdown. You can also create a new list directly from this dialog by tapping **Create New List**.

### When Editing

Open any subscription card and change the **List** field to reassign it. You can also remove the list assignment by deselecting the current list.

## Unassigned Subscriptions

Subscriptions without a list assignment are called "unassigned". These appear in:

- The **All** filter (along with all other subscriptions)
- The **Unassigned** filter chip

### Using the Unassigned Filter

The Unassigned filter chip makes it easy to find subscriptions that aren't organized into lists.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the filter dialog">
    On the Dashboard, tap the **List: All** chip below the chart.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Unassigned">
    Choose **Unassigned** from the filter options.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View unassigned subscriptions">
    Only subscriptions without a list assignment will be displayed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This is particularly useful when:

- You want to organize new subscriptions into lists
- A list was deleted and you need to reassign its subscriptions
- You prefer to keep some subscriptions uncategorized

## Searching Lists

In the Manage Lists screen, use the search bar to quickly find specific lists by name.

<Note>
  Lists are entirely optional. You can use Recur effectively without creating any lists—all subscriptions will simply appear in the "All" view.
</Note>
